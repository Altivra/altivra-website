How We Work

A transparent, founder-led process from discovery to deployment. No surprises, no handoffs, no bloat.

01 30 min

Discovery Call

Understanding your business

We start with a focused 30-minute call to understand your operations, pain points, and goals. This isn't a sales pitch — it's a working session.

What we cover:

  • Your current workflows and bottlenecks
  • Where your team spends the most time on manual work
  • Your existing systems (CRM, ERP, databases)
  • What success looks like for you

What you get:

  • Honest assessment of whether AI is right for your problem
  • Initial ideas on approach and feasibility
  • Clear next steps (or a polite "this isn't a fit")
Book Your Discovery Call
02 3-5 days

Scoping & Proposal

Defining the solution

If there's a fit, we dive deeper. We may request access to sample data, existing documentation, or a walkthrough of your current process.

What we deliver:

  • Solution Design: How the AI system will work
  • Integration Plan: How it connects to your existing tools
  • Timeline: Realistic milestones and delivery dates
  • Fixed Price: No hourly surprises, clear deliverables

Our commitment:

If we can't solve your problem effectively, we'll tell you. We'd rather say no than deliver something that doesn't work.

03 2-4 weeks

Build & Iterate

Founder-led development

This is where the magic happens. We build your custom AI system with constant communication and iteration.

How we work:

  • Weekly Updates: You'll never wonder what's happening
  • Early Demos: See progress before final delivery
  • Your Feedback: We iterate based on real input
  • Direct Access: Talk to the person building, not a project manager

What makes us different:

The founder who sold you is the one building. No outsourcing, no junior handoffs, no "let me check with the team."

04 1 week

Testing & Refinement

Making sure it works perfectly

Before going live, we rigorously test the system with real scenarios and edge cases from your business.

What we test:

  • Accuracy on your actual data
  • Edge cases and exceptions
  • Integration with your existing workflows
  • Performance under load

Your involvement:

We'll have your team test the system and provide feedback. Any issues get fixed before we call it done.

05 1-2 days

Deployment & Training

Going live with confidence

We deploy to production and make sure your team knows exactly how to use the system.

What's included:

  • Production Deployment: System goes live in your environment
  • Team Training: Hands-on session with your users
  • Documentation: Clear guides for ongoing use
  • Monitoring Setup: We'll know if something breaks
06 Ongoing

Support & Evolution

We don't disappear after delivery

Your business evolves, and your AI system should too. We offer ongoing support to keep things running smoothly.

Support options:

  • 30-Day Warranty: Included with every project — we fix any issues
  • Monthly Retainer: Ongoing improvements and new features
  • On-Demand Support: Call us when you need us

Our goal:

Build you something so good that you want to keep working with us — not because you're locked in.
