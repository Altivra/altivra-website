The Claims Processing Challenge

Manual processes create delays that cost money and customer trust

Document Overload

Every claim comes with photos, receipts, medical records, police reports — all requiring manual review and data entry.

Slow Processing

Claims sit in queues while adjusters work through backlogs. Simple claims that should take hours take days or weeks.

Inconsistent Decisions

Different adjusters make different decisions on similar claims. Lack of standardization creates compliance risk.

Manual Underwriting

Underwriters spend hours gathering data and assessing risk that could be automated, slowing down policy issuance.
